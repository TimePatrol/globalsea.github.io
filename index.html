Virtual AppSec Indonesia 2020

21th - 22th of November, 2020

Welcome to Virtual AppSec Indonesia 2020 - The Largest Application Security Conference in Indonesia and Southeast Asia (SEA) on November 21-22, 2020

The Virtual AppSec Indonesia 2020 is the premier application security conference for developers and security experts. Designed for private and public sector infosec professionals, the OWASP one day training and one day conference equips developers, defenders, and advocates to build a more secure web.

Join us for a celebration of leading application security technologies, speakers, prospects, and community, in a unique event that will build on everything you already know to expect from an AppSec Indonesia.

  • Join your Information Security and Developer running by virtual!
  • Training classes start November 22, with the keynotes, talks and CTFs starting November 21. The best speakers from around the world have been gathering at AppSec Indonesia. We guarantee you will leave the conference with lots of new knowledge and curiosity.
